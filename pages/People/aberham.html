Vito Aberham

Graduate Research Aide

Task 2

  Vito Aberham is a graduate student in astrophysics who stayed at ASU for the academic year 2023/24 as part of an exchange program with Heidelberg University. He is interested in star formation, general relativity and cosmology. His research had previously been on relativistic jets in active galactic nuclei. He joined Steve to work on Task 2 in 2023, covering modeling of both exoplanet atmospheres and the early evolution of the solar nebula. His main objective was elucidating the origin of CAI-rich asteroids as well as the formation of CI chondrites in the solar system.
