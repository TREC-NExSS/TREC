Austin Ware

Graduate Research Aide

Task 2

  Austin Ware is a graduate student at ASU working with Prof. Patrick Young (Co-Investigator). He is interested in stellar evolution of low mass (F-, G-, K-, M-type) stars, terrestrial (Earth-like) exoplanet climate modeling, and the habitability of terrestrial exoplanets. He is aiding Patrick Young with Task 2 to refine age estimates of terrestrial exoplanet hosts through the use of isochronal, gyrochronology, and stellar activity based methods.
