Prof. Hilairy Hartnett

Co-Investigator

Task 5

  Hilairy Hartnett is an oceanographer and biogeochemist whose research focuses on extreme environments and what makes them habitable. Those environments range from oceans to lakes, rivers, and hot springs as well as desert soils, urban environments, and potentially even exoplanetary environments. She is an interdisciplinarian who embraces a wide range of questions and analytical approaches in her science.
   She is leading the integrative and outward facing TREC task 5 activities that involve community workshops to gather input about important parameters for exoplanet modeling and to develop training modules to advance best practices for interdisciplinary teams.
