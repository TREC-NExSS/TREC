Meet the Team

Dr. Kara Brugman

Co-Investigator

Task 4 Lead

  Kara Brugman is an experimental petrologist who is interested in magma genesis and volatile solubility on exoplanets, Earth, and solar system bodies; the timescales of events in magmatic systems; geothermometry; volcano science and hazards; high-silica magmatic systems; and how our planet's volcanologic and petrologic mechanisms can be used to understand those on other planets and moons. Dr. Brugman is leading the Task 4 team which will conduct experiments to determine melting curves and bioessential element solubilities for hypothetical exoplanet compositions, and will model the rates of change of upper mantle and crust compositions and volatile contents as heat fluxes for these hypothetical exoplanets decline.
