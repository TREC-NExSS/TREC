Anneli "Lee" Sorensen

Undergraduate Research Aide

  Anneli “Lee” Sorensen is a sophomore at Arizona State University, studying Aerospace Astronautics Engineering with a minor in Astrophysics. She created this website Full Stash via HTML, CSS, and Javascript. She is actively involved in ASU’s NExSS TREC project, focusing on exoplanet composition, and through ASU/NASA Space Grant, contributes to research on the impact of Arctic ice on global warming. In addition, Lee is a member of ASU’s Combat Ready Robotics club, participates in the ASU Derby Devils team, and has organized K-12 STEM events, introducing robotics to over 100 students. She aspires to pursue a master’s degree in Astrophysics and a career in aerospace engineering research at NASA.
